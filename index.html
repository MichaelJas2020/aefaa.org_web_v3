About Us

Our Story

The school is part of the Agricultural Family School (EFA), and the main goal is to teach farming and offer differentiated education based on Alternating Pedagogy for rural students in the municipality of Angical. Currently, it receives students from various towns in the western region of Bahia.

 

 

Sponsors

The school has very little support from the local government. Our existence depends on donations from people like you. The average monthly cost per student is £55.

Students

We currently have 176 students who attend frequently, we have trained 218 young people in the vocational courses, plus hundreds of other elementary school students.

School

The school has to offer 4.5 hectares of land, which includes numerous gardens, trees, and vegetable plantations.
